Lasagna

This is a homemade lasagna recipe, it's a family special. The lasagna is made from fresh tomato sauce and is extremely filling and delicous. It is infact a traditional lasagna so the ingredients are very simple. This is perhaps the best lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. You mix the beef and the pork
  2. Stir the tomatoes that have been crushed with the tomato sauce and add the spices
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water
  4. mix cottage hceese and eggs and spices in a bowl
  5. assemble the lasagna
  6. bake the lasagna for 30 minutes and then remove and leave to cool for 15 minutes
